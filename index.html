ACM IGDTUW Student Chapter

We are the Association for Computing Machinery(ACM), Student Chapter, Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University For Women, or in short, IGDTUW-ACM. The student chapter in IGDTUW was established in 2012 and has chapter members from all subjects and of all years – freshmen to seniors. ACM @ IGDTUW is the technical student chapter of the Department of Information Technology, Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. Being the flagship student chapter at the Department of Information Technology, the chapter contributes immensely in Annual Technical Festival of University (Innerve). Since its inception, student chapter is being ably guided by Rishabh Kaushal, Assistant Professor as Faculty Advisor. Student Chapter has successfully hosted numerous highly useful events in the field of computing and helped students improve their skills and gain knowledge, thereby helping students in learning beyond academic curriculum.
